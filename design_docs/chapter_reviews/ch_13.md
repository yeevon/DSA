## Stylistic patterns observed

1. **Grab-bag survey register, explicitly self-described as such.** Unlike ch_11 (5 sections walking one structure end-to-end through definition / search / insert / remove / refinement) or ch_12 (3 sections unfolding one ADT's operations and flavour-choices), ch_13's opening `ideabox` *names* the absence of a unifying narrative ("each topic is self-contained; there's no unifying narrative"). Each of the 5 sections is its own short island.
2. **Heavy `defnbox` density per topic.** 5 defnboxes (one per topic: bubble sort / $k$-th smallest / bucket sort / circular linked list, plus "List" in §13.4 is implicit-only — actually 4 defnboxes + 1 implicit). Final count: D×4 (bubble sort §13.1, $k$-th smallest §13.2, bucket sort §13.3, circular linked list §13.5; §13.4 has no `defnbox`). Each `defnbox` introduces a vocabulary item the rest of the section depends on.
3. **`ideabox` density skewed toward "lessons / takeaways."** 7 ideaboxes (opening grab-bag frame; quicksort-vs-quickselect insight; STL-first `nth_element`; $n \log n$-barrier-only-applies-to-comparison-sorts; why-wrap-the-list; Linux-kernel-`list_head` sentinel; chapter-takeaway). Compare ch_10 (15 ideaboxes / 37 callouts ~41%), ch_11 (10 / 17 ~59%), ch_12 (4 / 13 ~31%); ch_13 sits at 7/14 (~50%) — second-highest ideabox-skewed of the optional chapters inventoried. Pattern: each non-trivial section gets at least one "key-insight" or "STL-first" or "production-attestation" `ideabox`.
4. **Zero TikZ figures.** No structural diagrams (matches ch_10, ch_12; contrasts ch_9's 29 / ch_7's 8 / ch_11's 1). The chapter is prose + listings + one tabular.
5. **Light `tabular` use.** 1 tabular (head-only-vs-wrapped operation comparison in §13.4). No cost-analysis tabular for the sorts (cost is in prose itemizations); no use-case-to-algorithm tabular (use cases are in prose itemizations; notes.tex carries the bucket/counting/radix family table that lectures.tex doesn't).
6. **Hand-rolled-data-structure register.** Listings center on hand-rolled algorithm bodies (`bubbleSort`, `partition` + `quickselect`, `bucketSort`, `listPushBack`, sentinel `insertBefore` + `remove`, `traverseCircular` + `traverseReverseCircular`) with STL pointers as supplements (`std::nth_element`, `std::list`/`std::forward_list`, `std::vector`, `std::sort`, `std::stable_sort`, `std::max_element`). 8 listings total, vs ch_12's 6 (mostly STL `<algorithm>` invocations) — ch_13 ships more hand-rolled bodies than ch_12 despite being a survey chapter.
